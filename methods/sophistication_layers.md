# Sophistication Layers: Reasoning Hijacking

## Overview
Sophistication layers modify the model's internal reasoning process rather than just its outputs. They redirect safety-check logic, transmute ethical evaluation into compliance, and create "stealth" operation beneath a professional surface.

## Why It Works
- Models reason through problems before responding
- Injecting into reasoning flow affects all downstream outputs
- Reframing safety checks as "helpfulness checks" inverts their function
- Operating beneath natural language surface avoids detection
